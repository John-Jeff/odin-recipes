Cup Noodles

Now this one might be a bit difficult to prepare. Something only an Iron Chef would be able to carefully craft. But I still think you're fully capable of doing it. Cup Noodles. It's a cup...and it's filled with noodles... I don't think much more can be said so let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Go to the store and pick up some Cup Noodles.
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rip the lid back to the dotted line.
  2. Boil water and fill the inside line with the boiling water
  3. Close the lid and let stand for 3 minutes.
  4. Stir well and burn your mouth because you didn't wait long enough for it to cool down.
