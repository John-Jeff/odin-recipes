Pizza Bagels

Description

Who doesn't love pizza? Who doesn't like bagels? Now, who doesn't love both of them combined. No one. That's exactly why you're here to learn how to make them. Obviously the frozen store bought ones aren't cutting it...I don't blame you. So just follow along with these easy steps to create some delicious homemade pizza bagels!!!

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you'll need:
  • 4 full-sized plain bagels, halved
  • 1/2 cup store-bought or homemade pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 ounces mini pepperoni
  • Dried basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Assemble the pizza bagels
    • On each bagel half, spread 1 tablespoon of pizza sauce 1 ounce of cheese and a few mini pepperonis. Lightly sprinkle with dried basil.
  3. Bake pizza bagels for 8 to 10 minutes
